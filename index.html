Menu Section

Hot Coffee

Rich and comforting, hot coffee is a classic favorite, brewed fresh and enjoyed black or with cream and sugar for a warm, energizing start to the day.

Espresso

A small, strong coffee brewed under pressure, espresso is the base for many other drinks like lattes and cappuccinos, providing a concentrated burst of rich flavor.

Cold Coffee

A creamy combination of espresso and steamed milk, lattes are smooth, mildly sweet, and a favorite choice for those who prefer a less intense coffee flavor.

Cappuccino

With equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, cappuccino is a rich, frothy coffee with a balanced taste, perfect for a morning indulgence.

Mocha

Combining espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, mocha offers a sweet, dessert-like coffee experience, perfect for chocolate lovers.

Americano

A diluted espresso with hot water, the Americano has a similar strength to brewed coffee but a more complex, bold flavor profile.
